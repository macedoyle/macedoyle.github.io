I'm Kenzie, a creative advertising designer and media photographer based in Michigan. I recently graduated from Michigan State University with a degree in Creative Advertising and a minor in Media Photography — and I've spent the last four years obsessing over the intersection of strategy and aesthetics.

I don't just make things look good. I make them make sense. Whether I'm concepting a full brand campaign, shooting a product on set, or editing a video for a real client, my goal is always the same: say something worth saying, in a way worth seeing.

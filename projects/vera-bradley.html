Vera
Bradley

2024 Brand Refresh Concept Project

Reimagining a beloved brand for a new generation.

Vera Bradley has always stood for color, personality, and handcrafted quality — but somewhere along the way, its visual identity fell behind the brands its audience actually gravitates toward. This project set out to answer one question: what would Vera Bradley look like if it dressed for who it wants to be?

The rebrand keeps the brand's warmth and storytelling DNA intact, while shifting its visual language toward something more editorial, more confident, and more lifestyle-driven. The result is a brand that feels like it belongs on the pages of a fashion magazine — without losing what makes it distinctly Vera Bradley.

The project spans a full editorial magazine spread (featuring Zooey Deschanel as brand ambassador) and a redesigned web experience — showing how the new direction translates across both print and digital touchpoints.

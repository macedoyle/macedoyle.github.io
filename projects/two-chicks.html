Product Photography

Two
Chicks

2024 Product Photography Set Design

Building a world around a product — from scratch.

Great product photography isn't about putting something in front of a camera. It's about constructing a feeling — a moment the viewer wants to step into. For Two Chicks, I built an entire set from the ground up: sourcing props, arranging compositions, and controlling every variable of lighting to create images that feel both editorial and shoppable.

Two Chicks makes ready-to-drink cocktails with a playful, feminine energy. The visual brief was to capture that lightness — summery, warm, alive — while making the product the undeniable hero of every frame. The result is a gallery of shots that could live comfortably in a paid social campaign, a brand deck, or on-shelf POS.

Video Production

MSU Board
of Trustees

2024 Videography & Editing Client Work — 42pointSEVEN

Making institutional storytelling actually worth watching.

Institutional video is one of the hardest genres to get right. The subject matter is serious, the audience is broad, and the temptation to make it dry is everywhere. This video — produced while I was working as a videographer for 42pointSEVEN — was a real-world test of how to make governance compelling.

The Michigan State University Board of Trustees plays a critical role in shaping the university's direction, but most students and community members have little sense of who they are or what they actually do. The goal of this video was to change that: to introduce the Board in a way that was clear, warm, and professionally polished.

I handled both filming and post-production — managing camera setup, on-location shooting, pacing in the edit, and audio mixing. The challenge was balancing clarity with engagement, and letting the people and the institution speak for themselves.

Campaign & Product Design

Kodak
Collective

2024 Brand Campaign Concept Project

Bringing a legacy brand into the era of the creative community.

Kodak has something most brands would kill for: genuine nostalgia. But nostalgia alone doesn't sell — especially to a generation that shoots on iPhones and posts to Instagram. This campaign asks: what if Kodak stopped living in the past and built a home for the people who still care about how a photo feels?

Kodak Collective is a digital + physical ecosystem that positions Kodak as the hub of a photography community — not just a film brand. The campaign includes a concept for a social platform where photographers share work tagged by camera, film stock, and location; a new physical product called the Pocket Print Plus Memory Kit; and a branded photo booth experience designed for events.

The strategy blends the tactile nostalgia of analog photography with the communal energy of modern social platforms, creating a brand world that feels both rooted and current.

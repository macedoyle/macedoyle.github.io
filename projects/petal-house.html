Brand & Experience Design

Petal
House

2024 Brand Identity Concept Project

Building a brand from the ground up — roots, walls, and all.

The best restaurant brands don't just describe food. They describe a feeling — a particular quality of light, the texture of a moment, the sensation of being somewhere that was made just for you. Petal House is a greenhouse-inspired fine dining concept built around exactly that feeling.

The brief was a blank slate: create a complete brand world for a restaurant that doesn't exist yet. I developed everything from the name logic and color palette to the menu system, visual language, and digital experience — designing every touchpoint as if Petal House were opening next month.

The result is a cohesive brand identity that communicates warmth, elevation, and a deep connection to natural beauty — without ever feeling rustic or precious. It's a place that earns a reservation.
